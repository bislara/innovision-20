About The Fest

Innovision, the annual techno-management fest of NIT Rourkela is organized in the first week of November spanning over three days and three nights. Commenced with a motto of providing technocrats all over the country a platform to showcase their technical skills and talent, Innovision has come a long way over the years since its inception.

It is one of the largest technical fests in the eastern region of the Indian subcontinent today which has made an impact on several college going students across the country. With different, captivating themes each year, the fest has recorded an immense participation in the past and this year will be no different.

Brace yourselves as the much awaited fest, INNOVISION 2K19 with ‘A Space Opera’ as its theme, takes off this November!

Where

NIT Rourkela, Sector-1, Rourkela-8, Odisha.

When

Friday to Sunday : 1-3 November

Innovision is the annual techno-management festival of NIT Rourkela organized by the students. It has been fuelling innovations and encouraging millions since 2004 and is a unique forum where the youth gets the opportunity to explore and experience science and technology in all its myriad forms. .
