NIT Rourkela (formerly REC Rourkela) is an institute of national importance, with UG, PG, and research programs in engineering and science. It is a publicly funded institute of higher learning for engineering and technology located in the steel city of Rourkela, Odisha, India. It is one of the 30 National Institutes of Technology in India and has been recognized as an Institute of National Importance by the National Institutes of Technology Act, 2007.
