FAQ

  • When is INNOVISION'19?

    Ans. INNOVISION,The annual techno-management festival of NIT Rourkela, will be held from 1st to 3rd of November.

  • How to register for INNOVISION'19?

    Ans. Please go to sign up option for registration

  • How much is the registration fee?

    Ans. You will get to know about the fee during registration process.

  • Will there be accommodation provided?

    Ans. Yes, accommodation will be provided to the participants in the various halls of residences located within our campus. After registering at our desk, our hospitality team would guide the participants to their allotted halls.

  • Do we have to pay entry fee for every events?

    Ans. Actually, there are events which have no entry fees but some events have. It differs for each event.

  • What is the refund policy if I don't want to join Innovision'19?

    Ans. The amount once paid can't be refunded under any circumstances.

  • Is the ticket transferable if I don't want to join Innovision'19?

    Ans. The tickets are non-transferable and non-refundable.

  • Is accomodation included in the registration fees?

    Ans. Yes, Innovision team will take care of the accomodation. However the participants will have to take care of the fooding on their own.


Innovision is the annual techno-management festival of NIT Rourkela organized by the students. It has been fuelling innovations and encouraging millions since 2004 and is a unique forum where the youth gets the opportunity to explore and experience science and technology in all its myriad forms. .

NIT Rourkela (formerly REC Rourkela) is an institute of national importance, with UG, PG, and research programs in engineering and science. It is a publicly funded institute of higher learning for engineering and technology located in the steel city of Rourkela, Odisha, India. It is one of the 30 National Institutes of Technology in India and has been recognized as an Institute of National Importance by the National Institutes of Technology Act, 2007.
