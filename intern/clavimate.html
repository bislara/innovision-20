NIT Rourkela (formerly REC Rourkela) is an institute of national importance, with UG, PG, and research programs in engineering and science. It is one of the 30 National Institutes of Technology in India and has been recognized as an Institute of National Importance by the National Institutes of Technology Act, 2007.
